WENDY

Son Seung-wan known professionally as Wendy, is a South Korean singer under ASND. She is best known as a member of the group and has established herself as a solo artist, radio host, and musical actress. She later launched her solo career with the mini album "Like Water" on April 5, 2021, becoming the first Red Velvet member to release a solo album. In January 2022 she joined GOT the Beat.
